$2.4M Annual Leakage Identified in a GBS Outsourcing Contract

A global enterprise approaching contract renewal wanted an independent view of their GBS relationship — no specific allegations, the relationship appeared functional. BEANZ ACCORD™ was applied across all six dimensions in five weeks: invoices reconciled against contracted headcount, rates independently benchmarked, SLA governance assessed. Three findings: headcount variance between contracted and verified capacity, rate escalations applied without contractual trigger, and SLA credits not claimed under existing terms.
