Viraj Mehta — Founder & Principal Advisor

Most consultants talk about GCCs.
Viraj has actually built one.

The story begins not in a consulting firm, but on the inside. Twenty years across three Fortune 500 organisations — Procter & Gamble, General Mills, Kraft Heinz — building, scaling, and fixing operations across three continents. Supply chains. GBS functions. Manufacturing transformations. Not advising from the outside. Carrying the P&L, the headcount targets, the attrition numbers, the board presentations.

"When the roots are strong,
the tree takes care of itself."

Then came the defining chapter: building Kraft Heinz's first-ever Global Capability Centre from a blank page. No template. No predecessor to follow. A mandate, a timeline, and accountability for the outcome. The result: 1,000+ capacity, 20+ Centres of Excellence, and under 5% attrition against an industry average of 20%.

That experience is not the backstory to Beanz Consulting. It is Beanz Consulting. Every framework, every recommendation, every engagement draws from what was learned — and what was earned — in those years. Not borrowed methodology. The real thing, with real accountability behind it.

Beanz Consulting exists because Viraj saw a gap the industry wasn't filling: independent advisory with no conflicts of interest, no staff augmentation business to protect, no vendor relationships to preserve. Just the practitioner's lens — on your side.
