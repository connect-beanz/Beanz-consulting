When a GBS outsourcing contract is signed, both parties are focused. Three years later, the same relationship often looks quite different — not through any single dramatic event, but through the slow accumulation of small divergences from what was originally agreed.

Finance can verify invoices are paid, but rarely has the delivery depth to challenge what is billed. Procurement negotiated the contract, then moved on. The result is a gap — sometimes modest, sometimes significant — between what was contracted, what is billed, and what is actually delivered.

BEANZ ACCORD™ is designed to surface that gap, on your terms, before the next renewal.
